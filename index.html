Biography
    Hi! My name is Jiafeng Hu. Currently, I am a software engineer in Google Shanghai. I recieved my Ph.D. degree from the department of computer science, The University of Hong Kong in 2018, supervised by Dr. Reynold Cheng , the M.Eng. degree from Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2014, supervised by Prof. Beihong Jin, and the B.Eng. degree from Jilin University in 2011.
